About us

The Botanical Gardens was established in 2015. Today the garden is popular with residens and visitors. The garden contains an important collection of plant species from all over the country. There is an onsite cafe with outdoor seating area. Opening times: Open daily from 9am to 6m with last admission 5:30pm
