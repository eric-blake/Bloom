Attractions

Visitor Center

The Visitor Centre is your first stop on any trip to Bloom Gardens, offering an exciting and innovative gateway to the Garden.

Plan your visit and get all the information you need to make sure your visit goes smoothly. Remember to pick up a leaflet at the reception to help you identify the various species.

The Visitor Centre is also where you can find the café an outdoor seacting area.

Walled Garden

The walled garen is open all year round, there’s something new to discover and admire here every season.

These tall walls have a strong aesthetic value to garden lovers. But they still retain their original and very valuable function, to raise the temperature of the garden and provide a more protective environment for the plants within.

Follow our self-guiding paths through the garden and learn more about its history and the restoration work that it took to return the garden to its former glory after falling into disrepair.

Bloom House

Bloom House is a Victorian mansion dating back to the mid 19th centrury. Building of the house began in 1845 and was completed in 1849.

There’s lots to see and do for visitors of all ages at Bloom Hosue. Enjoy a guided tour of the House and step back in time to the world of the 19th century.

Waterfall

A short, picturesque wooded walk offers varied viewing platforms to enjoy the waterfall.

Allow some time for your visit to view the magnificent spectacle, have a picnic and stroll around the acres of stunning countryside.
